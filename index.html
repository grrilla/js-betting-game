Betting Game

The Rules:

  • You must guess a number between 1 and 10 inclusive
  • You can bet either 5 or 10 chips
  • If you guess correctly, you win your bet
  • If you are +/- 1 of the correct value, you break even
  • If you are wrong, you lose your bet

Good luck!
